Mission

Technology

There is more technology available to golfers than ever before, but significant progress is still needed to make it accessible to everyone. We are interested in exploiting low cost sensors to help the golfer.

Data

Today's technology produces more data about the golfer and the golf swing than ever before, but often it is locked up in proprietary systems and formats. We are interested in changing this by developing open data standards.

Analytics

Analytical methods using data gathered from advanced technology are radicalizing every field around us. The golf world, however, is only scratching the surface of what can be done. We want to change this.

People

Ultimately, golfers are people and people's abilities, natural tendencies, and desires cover a broad spectrum. We want to make sure technology works for the individuals to best help them enjoy this great sport.
